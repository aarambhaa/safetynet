Platform capabilities

Every risk signal, one platform.

Built from the ground up for Nepal's unique risk landscape — combining government data feeds, satellite inputs, and ground truth from field operators.

01 //
🌩
Weather Intelligence

Hyperlocal forecasting from DHM Nepal, integrating satellite data for altitude-specific weather windows. Snowfall, wind, visibility, and precipitation alerts per route segment.

02 //
⚡
Seismic Monitoring

Real-time earthquake data from USGS and NSC Nepal. Magnitude thresholds, aftershock probability windows, and automated alerts to field coordinators within 90 seconds.

03 //
🛣
Route & Road Status

Landslide risk overlays, road blockage reports, and trail condition feeds crowdsourced from partner operators and government authorities across all major trekking corridors.

04 //
📊
Composite Risk Scoring

A probabilistic risk score (0–100) computed across all active signals for each location and group. Updated every 15 minutes. Fully auditable with factor breakdowns.

05 //
🚨
Alert Engine

Configurable thresholds trigger SMS, email, and in-app alerts to field coordinators. Escalation trees, on-call schedules, and two-way acknowledgement built in.

06 //
🤖
AI Field Assistant

Ask plain-language questions about any route, group, or condition. The AI synthesises live data into actionable recommendations — no dashboard expertise required.

Integrate risk data into your stack.

Available endpoints
GET /v1/risk/score
Composite risk score for any Nepal coordinate. Returns score, level, and factor breakdown.
GET /v1/weather/forecast
Altitude-corrected 72-hour weather forecast. Precipitation, snowfall probability, visibility, wind.
GET /v1/seismic/events
Real-time and historical earthquake events. Filter by magnitude, radius, and time window.
GET /v1/routes/status
Trail and road status for all major trekking corridors. Blockages, maintenance, and risk overlays.
POST /v1/alerts/subscribe
Subscribe a webhook or phone number to receive alerts for a specific group or location.
